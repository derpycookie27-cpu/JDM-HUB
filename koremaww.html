Popular JDM Models

Nissan Skyline GT-R R32

Performance & Handling

The R32 GT-R, known as "Godzilla," features a 2.6L RB26DETT twin-turbo inline-six engine, producing around 280 HP. It's renowned for its advanced ATTESA all-wheel-drive system and Super-HICAS four-wheel steering, offering exceptional handling and stability at high speeds.

  • Horsepower: 280 HP
  • 0-60 mph: ~5.6 seconds
  • Top Speed: 165 mph
  • Drive Type: AWD

Toyota Supra MK4

Performance & Handling

The MK4 Supra features a 3.0L 2JZ-GTE twin-turbo inline-six engine, famous for its durability and tuning potential, producing about 276 HP stock. It handles like a dream with a balanced chassis and rear-wheel drive, making it a favorite among car enthusiasts and tuners.

  • Horsepower: 276 HP
  • 0-60 mph: ~5.1 seconds
  • Top Speed: 155 mph
  • Drive Type: RWD

Mazda RX-7 FD3S

Performance & Handling

The RX-7 FD3S is powered by a 1.3L twin-rotor Wankel rotary engine, producing around 276 HP. Its lightweight design and precise steering make it a nimble and agile sports car, perfect for cornering at high speeds.

  • Horsepower: 276 HP
  • 0-60 mph: ~5.4 seconds
  • Top Speed: 165 mph
  • Drive Type: RWD
